Here you can find a listing of all my writings on the topic of Economics.

As much as Economists (and the media) would like everyone to believe that Economics is a hard science, based on things that can be represented as maths, that isn't exactly true.   
Economics will always be a social science, where things are simplified and abstracted in an attempt to understand and make sense of human behaviour.  In that process, the observer's own beliefs are always going to influence their analysis.  
You can't quite reach two opposite conclusions with the exact same data, but your thoughts on the theory can lead you to different interpretations of the same source data, and can possibly even reach different conclusions.

These writings therefore represent my opinion, my analysis.  
They are sorted by the last update date.
